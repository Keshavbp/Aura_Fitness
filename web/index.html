Mathematical Depth Heuristics

Aura Fitness tracks movement profiles in open space using isolated atan2 joint coordinate vertices:

  • Squat Depth Check: Tracks Hip-Knee-Ankle flexion angles continuously, registering repetitions only when parallel depth drops below 95°.
  • Push-up Sag Intercept: Calculates alignment deviation between Shoulder, Hip, and Ankle to identify spine sagging or high pikes.
  • Chest Fly Abduction: Monitors arm opening coordinates between 30° and 110° while tracking core stability.
