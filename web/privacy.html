Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

1. Overview

At AURA FITNESS, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in connection with our AI-powered squat and biomechanical form analysis mobile application and website.

2. On-Device Biometric Processing (Camera Feeds)

Our application requires access to your device's camera to enable real-time pose tracking (utilizing BlazePose landmark detection models).

CRITICAL INFO: Your video feed is processed entirely **on-device** in real-time. No video data, camera frames, or facial/skeletal telemetry logs are transmitted to or stored on external servers.

3. Account & Workout Telemetry Data

To maintain workout logs and track progress over time, we collect:

  • Basic account details (username, email, hashed credentials).
  • Workout history metrics (reps, calculated squat depths, angle statistics, velocity, and scoring parameters).

This data is saved locally first via your app's secure SQLite database and synced to our server backend asynchronously over encrypted TLS connections.

4. Security Practices

We implement rigorous security architectures to block unauthorized access to accounts, including database-backed IP rate limiting on login/registration gateways, password hashing using bcrypt algorithms, and secure SSL public key pinning within mobile client network adapters.

5. Your Data Rights

You retain full control over your workout statistics. You can request account deletion and the purge of your synced workout metrics by contacting our support team directly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect additions to our workout modules or changes in local-first database sync configurations.
